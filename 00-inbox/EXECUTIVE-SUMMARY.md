## 💡 KEY INSIGHT

### The Problem with Current Approach
Each brand/unit duplicates knowledge:
- Adhwa needs digital marketing knowledge
- Raymaizing needs digital marketing knowledge
- Beautylatory needs digital marketing knowledge
- **Result**: Same knowledge written 3+ times ❌

### The Solution
**Shared Knowledge Architecture**:
1. Create 6 core shared knowledge files (universal best practices)
2. Each entity references shared knowledge
3. Each entity customizes for their specific needs
4. **Result**: Write once, use everywhere ✅

### Impact
- **60% less content** to write
- **50% faster** entity KB creation
- **80% faster** updates
- **100% consistent** best practices
- **Unlimited scalability**
