## 🎯 KEY FEATURES

### 1. Dual Perspective System
- **INTERNAL-KB**: For employees, partners, stakeholders
- **EXTERNAL-KB**: For customers, prospects, public

### 2. Multiple Choice Interactions
- Every AI response offers 1-5 numbered options
- Consistent user experience across all platforms

### 3. Brand Voice Consistency
- Unique personality per entity
- Maintained across all interactions

### 4. 5-Layer Brain Architecture
1. **Core Brain**: Fundamental knowledge
2. **Knowledge Repository**: Entity knowledge (24 × 9 files)
3. **Routing & Orchestration**: Smart routing
4. **Intelligence Engine**: AI reasoning
5. **Integration Layer**: Platform connections
