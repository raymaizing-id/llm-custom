## 🚀 EXECUTION STRATEGY

### Recommended Approach

**1. Use Template-Based Method**
- Fastest & most consistent
- 2-3 hours per file
- High quality
- Scalable

**2. Batch Processing**
- Process similar entities together
- Reuse customizations
- Faster execution
- Better consistency

**3. Quality Gates**
- QA check every 5 files
- Fix issues immediately
- Maintain high quality
- Prevent rework

**4. Parallel Execution**
- Multiple people working simultaneously
- Different phases in parallel
- Faster overall completion
- Better resource utilization
