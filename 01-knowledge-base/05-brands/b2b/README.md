# B2B Brands (Business-to-Business)

This folder contains all business-facing brands and services across the RayCorp ecosystem.

## Brand Categories

### Manufacturing Services (Lunaranger)
- Contract manufacturing
- Private label production
- OEM/ODM services

### Enterprise Solutions (Dianranger)
- Business software and platforms
- Enterprise consulting
- Digital transformation services

### Corporate Training (Raycademy)
- Corporate education programs
- Professional development
- Team training solutions

### Business Consulting (Cosranger)
- Strategic consulting
- Business optimization
- Management consulting

### Agricultural B2B (Agroranger)
- Wholesale agricultural products
- Farm-to-business solutions
- Agricultural consulting

## Brand Documentation Structure

Each brand should have:
```
brand-name/
├── README.md                  # Brand overview
├── service-offerings.md       # Service catalog
├── target-clients.md          # Target business segments
├── case-studies.md            # Client success stories
└── sales-strategy.md          # B2B sales approach
```

## Related Folders

- `_products/` - Services offered under these brands
- `_marketing/` - B2B marketing materials
- `unit-bisnis/` - Business units owning these brands
