## 🤝 INFLUENCER MARKETING

### Influencer Tiers

**Tier 1: Macro Influencers** (100K+ followers)
- Budget: Rp 15-25 juta per campaign
- Quantity: 3-4 per year
- Focus: Brand awareness
- Examples: Hijabi celebrities, Ustadzah influencers

**Tier 2: Mid Influencers** (50K-100K followers)
- Budget: Rp 5-10 juta per campaign
- Quantity: 8-10 per year
- Focus: Engagement & conversion
- Examples: Hijabi beauty bloggers

**Tier 3: Micro Influencers** (10K-50K followers)
- Budget: Rp 1-3 juta per campaign
- Quantity: 20-30 per year
- Focus: Authentic reviews & community
- Examples: Muslimah content creators

### Influencer Selection Criteria
✅ Must be hijabi
✅ Islamic values alignment
✅ Authentic engagement
✅ Target audience match
✅ Content quality
✅ Previous halal brand collaborations
