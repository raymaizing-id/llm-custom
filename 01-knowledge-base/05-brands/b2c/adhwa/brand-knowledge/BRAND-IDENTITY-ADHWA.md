## 🎯 BRAND ESSENCE

**Brand Name**: Adhwa (أضواء)
**Meaning**: "Lights" in Arabic - representing guidance, clarity, and illumination
**Tagline**: "Kecantikan yang Diberkahi" / "Blessed Beauty"

**Brand Promise**: 
Halal certified beauty products that honor Islamic values while delivering premium quality results.

## 🎨 VISUAL IDENTITY

### Logo
**Primary Logo**: Adhwa wordmark with Islamic geometric pattern
**Symbol**: Crescent moon with light rays
**Variations**: 
- Full color (primary)
- White (on dark backgrounds)
- Black (on light backgrounds)
- Icon only (for small spaces)

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors**:
- Emerald Green: #2D7A5F (main brand color)
  Represents: Growth, nature, halal, Islamic
  
- Cream: #F5F1E8 (secondary)
  Represents: Purity, cleanliness, natural

**Secondary Colors**:
- Gold: #C9A961 (accent)
  Represents: Premium, quality, blessing
  
- Soft Pink: #E8C4C4 (feminine touch)
  Represents: Beauty, care, gentleness

**Neutral Colors**:
- Dark Brown: #3E2723 (text)
- Light Gray: #F5F5F5 (backgrounds)
- White: #FFFFFF

### Typography

**Primary Font**: Montserrat
- Headings: Montserrat Bold
- Body: Montserrat Regular
- Modern, clean, readable

**Secondary Font**: Arabic Typesetting
- For Arabic text
- Islamic aesthetic
- Cultural connection

### Photography Style

**Models**:
- Hijabi women (proper hijab)
- Age range: 20-45
- Diverse skin tones
- Natural, authentic expressions

**Settings**:
- Natural light
- Soft, warm tones
- Clean, minimal backgrounds
- Islamic-inspired elements (subtle)

**Mood**:
- Warm & welcoming
- Modest & elegant
- Natural & authentic
- Peaceful & serene

## 🎯 BRAND POSITIONING

**Target Audience**: Muslim women 20-45, hijabis, halal-conscious

**Positioning Statement**:
"Untuk muslimah Indonesia yang ingin merawat kecantikan dengan cara yang halal dan berkualitas, Adhwa adalah brand skincare & kosmetik halal premium yang menawarkan produk tersertifikasi MUI dengan hasil nyata, karena kami percaya kecantikan sejati adalah yang diberkahi Allah."

**Competitive Advantage**:
- Halal certified (not just halal-friendly)
- Premium quality at accessible price
- Strong Islamic values integration

- Authentic care for customers
