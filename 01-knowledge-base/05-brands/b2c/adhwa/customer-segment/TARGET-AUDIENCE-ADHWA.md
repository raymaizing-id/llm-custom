# 🎯 Adhwa - Target Audience & Customer Segments

> **Brand**: Adhwa  
> **Document**: Customer Segmentation & Targeting  
> **Purpose**: Define and understand target customers  
> **Use**: Marketing, Product Development, Sales Strategy

---

## 👥 PRIMARY TARGET AUDIENCE

### Demographics
- **Gender**: Female
- **Age**: 20-45 years (primary: 25-35)
- **Religion**: Muslim (practicing)
- **Marital Status**: Mix (single, married, with/without children)
- **Location**: Urban areas (Jakarta, Surabaya, Bandung, Medan, Makassar)
- **Income**: Rp 5-20 juta/month
- **Education**: High school - University
- **Occupation**: Professionals, entrepreneurs, homemakers

### Psychographics
- **Values**: Islamic values, halal lifestyle, modesty
- **Lifestyle**: Active, social, community-oriented
- **Interests**: Islamic studies, beauty, fashion (modest), family
- **Behavior**: Halal-conscious, quality-seeking, community-engaged
- **Media**: Social media active, Islamic content consumers

### Religious Practice
- **Hijab**: Wears hijab (primary segment)
- **Prayer**: 5 times daily
- **Halal Awareness**: High (checks halal certification)
- **Islamic Events**: Participates in Ramadan, Eid, kajian
- **Community**: Active in Muslim communities

## 🎯 CUSTOMER SEGMENTS

### Segment 1: Young Professional Hijabis (30%)

**Profile**:
- Age: 23-32
- Occupation: Corporate, startup, professional
- Income: Rp 7-15 juta/month
- Status: Single or newly married

**Characteristics**:
- Career-focused but maintains Islamic values
- Social media savvy
- Trend-aware (modest fashion)
- Quality-conscious
- Willing to invest in good products

**Needs**:
- Effective skincare (busy lifestyle)
- Halal certified products
- Professional appearance
- Confidence boost
- Time-efficient routine

**Pain Points**:
- Limited time for complex routines
- Stress affecting skin
- Finding halal premium products
- Balancing career & Islamic values

**Buying Behavior**:
- Research before buying
- Read reviews extensively
- Influenced by hijabi influencers
- Online shopping preferred
- Willing to pay premium for quality

**Marketing Approach**:
- Instagram & TikTok focus
- Influencer partnerships
- Career woman + hijabi positioning
- Efficiency & results messaging
- Professional packaging

---

### Segment 2: Muslim Mothers (25%)

**Profile**:
- Age: 28-40
- Occupation: Homemaker or working mom
- Income: Household Rp 10-25 juta/month
- Status: Married with children

**Characteristics**:
- Family-oriented
- Practical & value-conscious
- Community-active (pengajian, arisan)
- Loyal to trusted brands
- Word-of-mouth influential

**Needs**:
- Safe products (for self & family)
- Halal assurance
- Simple routine
- Value for money
- Multi-purpose products

**Pain Points**:
- Limited me-time
- Skin issues post-pregnancy
- Budget considerations
- Finding safe, halal products
- Maintaining self-care with kids

**Buying Behavior**:
- Recommendations from friends
- Community influence strong
- Bundle/package preferred
- Offline & online mix
- Loyalty program important

**Marketing Approach**:
- WhatsApp community groups
- Mom influencers
- Family values messaging
- Bundle deals
- Community events (kajian + beauty)

## 🎯 CUSTOMER JOURNEY BY SEGMENT

### Awareness Stage
**Young Professional**: Instagram ads, influencer content
**Muslim Mothers**: Community recommendations, WhatsApp groups
**Hijrah Generation**: Islamic content, educational posts
**Established**: Premium publications, exclusive events
**Influencers**: Industry events, brand outreach

### Consideration Stage
**Young Professional**: Reviews, influencer demos, website
**Muslim Mothers**: Friend testimonials, community feedback
**Hijrah Generation**: Educational content, halal certification
**Established**: Product quality, brand reputation
**Influencers**: Brand values, product testing

### Decision Stage
**Young Professional**: Online purchase, fast delivery
**Muslim Mothers**: Bundle deals, value packages
**Hijrah Generation**: Starter sets, affordable options
**Established**: Premium products, VIP service
**Influencers**: Partnership terms, community value

### Retention Stage
**Young Professional**: Loyalty points, exclusive access
**Muslim Mothers**: Community engagement, family benefits
**Hijrah Generation**: Education, community support
**Established**: VIP treatment, personalized service
**Influencers**: Ongoing partnership, community impact
