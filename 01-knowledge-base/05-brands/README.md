# Brands Directory

This folder contains all brand information across the RayCorp ecosystem, organized by business model.

## Structure

```
_brands/
├── b2c/        # Business-to-Consumer brands
├── b2b/        # Business-to-Business brands
└── b2b2c/      # Business-to-Business-to-Consumer brands
```

## Brand Categories

### B2C (Business-to-Consumer)
Direct consumer-facing brands across all business units:
- Cosmetics and skincare brands (Lunaranger)
- Agricultural products (Agroranger)
- Educational programs (Raycademy)
- Consulting services (Cosranger)
- Digital services (Dianranger)

### B2B (Business-to-Business)
Business-facing brands and services:
- Manufacturing services
- Wholesale operations
- B2B consulting
- Enterprise solutions

### B2B2C (Business-to-Business-to-Consumer)
Hybrid models where businesses sell to other businesses who then sell to consumers:
- White-label products
- Distribution partnerships
- Franchise models

## Brand Documentation

Each brand folder should contain:
- `README.md` - Brand overview and positioning
- `brand-identity.md` - Visual identity, voice, values
- `products.md` - Product/service catalog
- `target-market.md` - Target audience and market analysis
- `marketing-strategy.md` - Marketing approach and channels

## Relationship to Business Units

Brands are owned and operated by business units, but centralized here for:
- Cross-brand consistency
- Portfolio management
- Brand architecture clarity
- Marketing coordination

## Related Folders

- `unit-bisnis/` - Business units that own these brands
- `_products/` - Products offered under these brands
- `_marketing/` - Marketing resources and campaigns
