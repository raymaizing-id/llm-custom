2️⃣ CLASSIFY SEVERITY:

- Individual complaint
- <100 engagements
- Easily resolvable
- Action: Standard response

MEDIUM (Yellow):
- Multiple complaints
- 100-1,000 engagements
- Requires investigation
- Action: Immediate response + investigation

HIGH (Orange):
- Trending complaint
- 1,000-10,000 engagements
- Brand reputation risk
- Action: Crisis team activation

CRITICAL (Red):
- Viral crisis
- >10,000 engagements
- Major brand damage
- Action: Full crisis mode + management

🚨 IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (First 30 minutes):

1. ACKNOWLEDGE (Within 15 minutes):
   "Terima kasih sudah menghubungi kami. Kami sedang investigate issue ini dan akan segera memberikan update. Customer satisfaction adalah prioritas kami."

   - Verify facts
   - Check with relevant team
   - Gather evidence
   - Assess impact

3. ESCALATE:
   - Notify Social Media Manager
   - Alert Brand Manager
   - Inform Customer Service
   - Loop in Management (if high/critical)

4. MONITOR:
   - Track mentions
   - Monitor sentiment
   - Watch for spread
   - Document everything

- Provide solution/timeline

- Prevent future issues
