## 🎯 DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

**Finance** adalah department yang bertanggung jawab atas financial planning, accounting, budgeting, financial reporting, dan treasury management untuk memastikan kesehatan finansial perusahaan dan mendukung decision making yang berbasis data finansial.

**Mission**: Ensure financial health and enable data-driven business decisions

## 🔄 KEY PROCESSES

### Monthly Closing Process
**Day 1-5**: Transaction cut-off & reconciliation
**Day 6-10**: Journal entries & adjustments
**Day 11-15**: Financial statements preparation
**Day 16-20**: Review & approval
**Day 21-25**: Reporting & analysis

### Budgeting Process
**Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Budget preparation
- Guidelines issuance
- Department submissions
- Consolidation
- Review & adjustments

**Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Budget finalization
- Management review
- Board approval
- Communication
- System setup

### Payment Process
**Step 1**: Payment request submission
**Step 2**: Verification & approval
**Step 3**: Payment processing
**Step 4**: Documentation & recording
