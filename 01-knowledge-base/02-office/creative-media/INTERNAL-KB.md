## 📋 STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES

### SOP 1: Creative Brief Process
1. Receive brief from stakeholder
2. Review & clarify requirements (24 hours)
3. Assign team & resources
4. Schedule kickoff meeting
5. Confirm timeline & deliverables
6. Begin creative process

**SLA**: Brief review within 24 hours

---

### SOP 2: Design Approval Process
1. Designer creates initial concepts (2-3 options)
2. Internal review (Art Director)
3. Present to stakeholder
4. Gather feedback
5. Revisions (max 3 rounds)
6. Final approval
7. File preparation & delivery

**SLA**: 
- Concept presentation: 3-5 days
- Revision turnaround: 1-2 days
- Final delivery: 1 day after approval

---

### SOP 3: Photography Project Process
1. Receive photography brief
2. Plan shoot (location, props, models)
3. Schedule shoot
4. Conduct photoshoot
5. Photo selection
6. Editing & retouching
7. Delivery

**SLA**: 
- Planning: 2-3 days
- Shoot: 1 day
- Editing: 3-5 days
- Total: 1-2 weeks

---

### SOP 4: File Management
1. Organize files by project
2. Use naming convention: [Brand]_[Project]_[Asset]_[Version]_[Date]
3. Save working files in project folder
4. Export final files in required formats
5. Archive completed projects
6. Backup weekly
