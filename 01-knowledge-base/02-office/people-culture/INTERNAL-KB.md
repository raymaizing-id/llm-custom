## 🎯 DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

**People & Culture** adalah department yang bertanggung jawab atas talent acquisition, employee development, performance management, compensation & benefits, employee engagement, dan organizational culture untuk memastikan perusahaan memiliki tim yang capable, engaged, dan aligned dengan company values.

**Mission**: Build a high-performing, engaged workforce that drives business success

## 🔄 KEY PROCESSES

### Recruitment Process

**Week 1: Requisition & Planning**
- Hiring request approval
- Job description finalization
- Sourcing strategy
- Budget confirmation

**Week 2-3: Sourcing & Screening**
- Job posting (internal & external)
- Candidate sourcing
- CV screening
- Phone screening
- Assessment tests

**Week 4-5: Interviewing**
- HR interview
- Hiring manager interview
- Team interview
- Technical assessment (if needed)
- Reference check

**Week 6: Offer & Onboarding**
- Offer preparation
- Negotiation
- Offer acceptance
- Background check
- Onboarding preparation

---

### Onboarding Process

**Pre-Day 1**:
- Welcome email
- Documentation preparation
- IT setup
- Workspace preparation
- Buddy assignment

**Day 1**:
- Welcome & orientation
- Company overview
- Policies & procedures
- IT setup
- Team introduction

**Week 1**:

- Role training
- System access
- Initial assignments
- Check-in meetings

**Month 1**:
- Role immersion
- Training programs
- Performance expectations
- 30-day review

**Month 3**:
- Probation review
- Performance assessment
- Career discussion
- Confirmation/extension

### Performance Review Process

**Preparation Phase** (Week 1-2):
- Self-assessment
- Manager assessment
- Peer feedback (360)
- Goal review

**Review Meeting** (Week 3):
- Performance discussion

**Follow-up** (Week 4):

- Development plan creation
- Compensation review
