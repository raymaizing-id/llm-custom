## 📋 CONTENT TYPES & PRODUCTION

**Volume**: 40 articles/month
**Length**: 800-2,000 words

**Process**:

4. Editing (1 hour)

- Email newsletters

## 📋 STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES

### SOP 1: Content Request Process
1. Stakeholder submits request (form)
2. Content team reviews (24 hours)
3. Feasibility assessment
4. Timeline & resource allocation
5. Approval & kickoff
6. Production
7. Review & revisions
8. Delivery

**SLA**: 5 business days (standard), 2 days (urgent)

---

### SOP 2: Content Approval Process
1. Creator submits for review
2. Peer review (quality check)
3. Manager approval (brand compliance)
4. Stakeholder approval (if needed)
5. Final approval & publishing

**SLA**: 24 hours (standard), 4 hours (urgent)

---

### SOP 3: Content Publishing Process
1. Final content ready
2. Schedule in publishing tool
3. Double-check (time, platform, copy)
4. Publish
5. Monitor first hour
6. Engage with comments
7. Track performance
