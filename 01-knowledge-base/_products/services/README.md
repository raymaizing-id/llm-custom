# Service Offerings

This folder contains all service offerings across multiple business units.

## Service Categories

### Educational Services (Raycademy)
- Training programs
- Courses and workshops
- Certification programs

### Consulting Services (Cosranger)
- Strategic consulting
- Business optimization
- Management consulting

### Digital Services (Dianranger)
- Software development
- Digital transformation
- Platform services

### Manufacturing Services (Lunaranger)
- Contract manufacturing
- Private label production
- OEM/ODM services

## Documentation

Each service should include:
- Service description
- Deliverables
- Pricing models
- Target clients
- Brand association
- Business unit owner
