# Products Directory

This folder contains comprehensive product information across all business units and brands in the RayCorp ecosystem.

## Structure

```
_products/
├── cosmetics/      # Cosmetic products (Lunaranger)
├── skincare/       # Skincare products (Lunaranger)
├── agriculture/    # Agricultural products (Agroranger)
├── services/       # Service offerings (all units)
└── example-product.md
```

## Product Categories

### Cosmetics (Lunaranger)
- Makeup products
- Color cosmetics
- Beauty tools and accessories

### Skincare (Lunaranger)
- Facial care
- Body care
- Treatment products

### Agriculture (Agroranger)
- Organic products
- Agricultural solutions
- Farm-to-table offerings

### Services (Multiple Units)
- Educational services (Raycademy)
- Consulting services (Cosranger)
- Digital services (Dianranger)

## Product Documentation

Each product should include:
- Product name and SKU
- Description and benefits
- Ingredients/specifications
- Pricing and variants
- Target market
- Brand association
- Business unit owner

## Cross-Reference

Products are:
- **Owned by**: Business units (`unit-bisnis/`)
- **Sold under**: Brands (`_brands/`)
- **Marketed through**: Marketing campaigns (`_marketing/`)

## Template

Use `example-product.md` as a template for creating new product documentation.

## Related Folders

- `unit-bisnis/` - Business units that develop products
- `_brands/` - Brands under which products are sold
- `_marketing/` - Marketing materials for products
