## 🎯 DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

**Production** adalah department yang bertanggung jawab atas manufacturing operations, production planning, batch execution, dan output delivery untuk memastikan produksi kosmetik berjalan efisien, on-time, dan sesuai dengan quality standards yang ditetapkan.

**Mission**: Deliver quality products on time through efficient manufacturing operations

## 🔄 PRODUCTION PROCESS

### Pre-Production
**Day -2**:
- Production order review
- Material verification
- Equipment preparation
- Line clearance
- Documentation preparation

**Day -1**:
- Final material check
- Equipment calibration
- Team briefing
- Safety check

### Production
**Phase 1: Setup** (2 hours)
- Equipment setup
- Material staging
- Line clearance verification
- Safety check

**Phase 2: Mixing** (4-6 hours)
- Raw material weighing
- Mixing process
- In-process testing
- Temperature monitoring
- pH monitoring

**Phase 3: Filling** (6-8 hours)
- Filling operation
- Weight check
- Visual inspection
- Packaging
- Labeling

**Phase 4: Completion** (2 hours)
- Final inspection
- Batch documentation
- Cleaning
- Storage

### Post-Production
- Batch record review
- Quality release
- Inventory update
- Equipment cleaning
- Area clearance
