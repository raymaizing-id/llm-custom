## 🎯 DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

**Research & Development** adalah department yang bertanggung jawab atas formula development, product innovation, ingredient research, dan technical support untuk mengembangkan produk-produk baru yang innovative, effective, dan market-competitive untuk semua brand dan unit bisnis.

**Mission**: Drive innovation through scientific excellence and market insight

## 🔄 PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT PROCESS

**Phase 1: Briefing** (Week 1)
- Project brief
- Market research
- Competitive analysis
- Target profile definition
- Timeline & budget

**Phase 2: Concept Development** (Week 2-3)
- Ingredient selection
- Formula design
- Initial prototypes (3-5 options)
- Preliminary testing
- Concept presentation

**Phase 3: Optimization** (Week 4-6)
- Formula refinement
- Stability testing
- Performance testing
- Cost optimization
- Packaging compatibility

**Phase 4: Validation** (Week 7-8)
- Final formula confirmation
- Comprehensive testing
- Scale-up trial
- Documentation
- Approval

**Phase 5: Transfer** (Week 9-10)
- Manufacturing transfer
- Training
- First production batch
- Technical support
- Documentation handover
