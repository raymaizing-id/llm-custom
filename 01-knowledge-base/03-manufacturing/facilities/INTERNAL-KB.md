## 🎯 DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

**Facilities Management** adalah department yang bertanggung jawab atas building maintenance, utilities management, security, cleaning services, dan general facility operations untuk memastikan safe, comfortable, dan efficient working environment.

**Mission**: Provide world-class facility services that enable operational excellence
