## 🎯 DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

**PPIC** adalah department yang bertanggung jawab atas production planning, inventory control, material requirements planning (MRP), dan production scheduling untuk memastikan production efficiency, material availability, dan on-time delivery dengan optimal resource utilization.

**Mission**: Orchestrate seamless production flow through intelligent planning and control

## 🔄 PLANNING PROCESS

### Monthly Planning Cycle

**Week 1: Demand Consolidation**
- Collect sales forecasts (all brands)
- Review customer orders
- Check stock levels
- Analyze trends
- Consolidate requirements

**Week 2: Capacity Planning**
- Calculate production capacity
- Identify constraints
- Plan resource allocation
- Schedule maintenance
- Optimize utilization

**Week 3: Material Planning**
- Run MRP
- Generate purchase requisitions
- Coordinate with suppliers
- Confirm lead times
- Plan safety stock

**Week 4: Schedule Finalization**
- Create master schedule
- Coordinate with production
- Communicate to stakeholders
- Prepare documentation
- Monitor execution

### Weekly Planning Cycle

**Monday**:
- Review weekly targets
- Confirm material availability
- Finalize daily schedules
- Brief production teams
- Set priorities

**Tuesday-Thursday**:
- Monitor execution
- Track progress
- Resolve issues
- Adjust schedules
- Update stakeholders

**Friday**:
- Week-end review
- Performance analysis
- Next week preparation
- Report to management
- Continuous improvement
