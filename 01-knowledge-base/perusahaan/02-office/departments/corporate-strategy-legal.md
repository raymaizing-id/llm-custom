## Key Processes

### Strategic Planning Process
1. **Environmental Scan** - Market and competitor analysis
2. **SWOT Analysis** - Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
3. **Strategy Formulation** - Develop strategic initiatives
4. **Implementation Planning** - Action plans and timelines
5. **Monitoring & Review** - Quarterly strategy reviews

### Contract Management Process
1. **Request** - Contract request from business units
2. **Drafting** - Legal drafting or review
3. **Negotiation** - Terms negotiation
4. **Approval** - Management approval
5. **Execution** - Signing and filing
6. **Monitoring** - Contract performance tracking

### Licensing Process
1. **Requirements Check** - Identify required licenses
2. **Documentation** - Prepare required documents
3. **Submission** - Submit to authorities
4. **Follow-up** - Track application status
5. **Issuance** - Receive and file licenses
6. **Renewal Tracking** - Monitor expiry dates
