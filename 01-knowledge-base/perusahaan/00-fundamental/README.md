# Fundamental Knowledge Base

This folder contains the foundational knowledge that defines the RayCorp ecosystem, including organizational structure, AI frameworks, and core principles.

## Structure

```
_fundamental/
├── organizational-structure/   # Company structure, hierarchy, business units
├── ai-hierarchy/              # AI Digital Employee structure (4-level hierarchy)
├── frameworks/                # VCORETEX Framework, protocols, guidelines
└── archives/                  # Historical and raw fundamental knowledge
```

## Contents

### Organizational Structure
- Master company structure (RayCorp holding + 5 business units)
- 3-tier organizational model
- Business unit definitions and relationships
- Legal entity information

### AI Hierarchy
- **Level 1**: RANGERRAY (Super-Superbot)
- **Level 2**: Sub-Superbot (per business unit)
- **Level 3**: Superbot Divisi (per division)
- **Level 4**: Otobot (operational bots)

### Frameworks
- **VCORETEX Framework**: Core AI interaction framework
- **Command Protocol**: Interaction guidelines
- **Character Identity**: AI personality and voice
- **User Voice Tone**: Communication standards

### Archives
- Raw fundamental knowledge
- Historical documentation
- Legacy frameworks and references

## Purpose

This folder serves as the **single source of truth** for:
- How RayCorp is structured
- How AI systems are organized
- How communication should flow
- Core principles and frameworks

## Usage

All business units, divisions, and AI bots should reference these fundamental documents to ensure consistency and alignment with core principles.
