## Services

### 1. Full OEM/Maklon
**Description**: Manufaktur produk dari formula hingga packaging  
**Includes**:
- Formula development
- Raw material sourcing
- Production
- Quality control
- Packaging
- Labeling
- BPOM registration support

**MOQ**: 500-1000 pcs (depending on product type)

### 2. Private Label
**Description**: Menggunakan formula existing dengan brand client  
**Includes**:
- Production using existing formula
- Custom packaging & labeling
- Quality control
- BPOM registration support

**MOQ**: 300-500 pcs

### 3. Contract Manufacturing
**Description**: Produksi dengan formula dari client  
**Includes**:
- Production only
- Quality control
- Packaging (optional)

**MOQ**: 1000 pcs

### 4. Product Development
**Description**: Pengembangan produk baru  
**Includes**:
- Formula development
- Stability testing
- Safety testing
- Sample production
- Regulatory consultation

**Timeline**: 3-6 months

## Process & Timeline

### Standard Process
1. **Consultation** (1-2 weeks)
   - Discuss product concept
   - Define specifications
   - Provide quotation

2. **Formula Development** (4-8 weeks)
   - Develop formula
   - Create samples
   - Client approval

3. **Testing** (4-12 weeks)
   - Stability testing
   - Safety testing
   - Efficacy testing (optional)

4. **Registration** (3-6 months)
   - BPOM registration
   - Halal certification (optional)

5. **Production** (2-4 weeks)
   - Raw material procurement
   - Production
   - Quality control
   - Packaging

6. **Delivery** (1 week)
   - Final inspection
   - Delivery to client
