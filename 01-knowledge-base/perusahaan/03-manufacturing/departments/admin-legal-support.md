## Factory Licenses & Permits

### Business Licenses
- **TDI**: Tanda Daftar Industri (Industry Registration)
- **IUI**: Izin Usaha Industri (Industrial Business License)
- **SIUP**: Surat Izin Usaha Perdagangan (Trading Business License)
- **NIB**: Nomor Induk Berusaha (Business Identification Number)

### Product Licenses
- **BPOM**: Product registration for all products
- **Halal**: MUI Halal certification
- **CPKB**: Cara Pembuatan Kosmetik yang Baik certificate
- **CPOTB**: Cara Pembuatan Obat Tradisional yang Baik certificate

### Environmental Permits
- **UKL-UPL**: Environmental management and monitoring
- **SPPL**: Environmental management statement
- **Wastewater Permit**: Wastewater discharge permit

### Building & Safety
- **IMB**: Building permit
- **SLF**: Building safety certificate
- **Fire Safety**: Fire safety certificate
