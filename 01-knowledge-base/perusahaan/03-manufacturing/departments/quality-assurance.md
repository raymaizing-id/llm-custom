# Quality Assurance

## Quick Facts
- **Type**: Manufacturing Department
- **Parent**: Lunaray Beauty Factory
- **Head**: Head of Quality Assurance
- **Team Size**: 8-10 people
- **Status**: ✅ Active

## Department Overview

### Vision
Memastikan setiap produk yang keluar dari Lunaray Beauty Factory memenuhi standar kualitas tertinggi.

### Mission
- Ensure product quality and safety
- Maintain compliance with regulations (BPOM, GMP)
- Implement quality control systems
- Continuous quality improvement
- Zero defect goal

### Core Functions
1. **Quality Control** - In-process and final product testing
2. **Quality Assurance** - System and process quality
3. **Testing & Analysis** - Lab testing and analysis
4. **Documentation** - Batch records and quality documentation
5. **Compliance** - Regulatory compliance management

## Quality Control Process

### 1. Raw Material QC
- **Incoming Inspection**: Visual, physical, chemical tests
- **Certificate of Analysis (CoA)**: Verification
- **Sampling**: Representative sampling
- **Testing**: Identity, purity, quality tests
- **Approval/Rejection**: Accept or reject decision

### 2. In-Process QC
- **Line Clearance**: Pre-production check
- **Process Monitoring**: pH, viscosity, temperature
- **Sampling**: Regular in-process sampling
- **Testing**: Compliance with specifications
- **Corrective Actions**: If deviations found

### 3. Final Product QC
- **Physical Tests**: Appearance, color, odor, texture
- **Chemical Tests**: pH, viscosity, specific gravity
- **Microbiological Tests**: Total plate count, pathogens
- **Stability Tests**: Accelerated and real-time
- **Packaging Tests**: Seal integrity, labeling accuracy

### 4. Release Testing
- **Final Review**: All test results reviewed
- **Batch Record Review**: Complete documentation check
- **Release Decision**: Approve or reject for release
- **Certificate of Analysis**: Issue CoA for approved batches

## Testing Laboratory

### Physical-Chemical Lab
- **Equipment**: pH meter, viscometer, refractometer, spectrophotometer
- **Tests**: pH, viscosity, specific gravity, color, odor

### Microbiological Lab
- **Equipment**: Incubators, autoclaves, laminar flow, microscopes
- **Tests**: Total plate count, yeast & mold, pathogens (E.coli, S.aureus, P.aeruginosa)

### Stability Chamber
- **Conditions**: 40°C/75%RH (accelerated), 30°C/65%RH (intermediate), 25°C/60%RH (long-term)
- **Duration**: 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, 24 months

## Quality Standards

### Regulatory Compliance
- **BPOM**: Badan Pengawas Obat dan Makanan
- **CPKB**: Cara Pembuatan Kosmetik yang Baik (GMP)
- **Halal**: MUI Halal Certification
- **ISO 9001**: Quality Management System

### Product Specifications
- All products have detailed specifications
- Specifications include physical, chemical, microbiological parameters
- Specifications approved by R&D and QA
