## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Equipment Maintenance
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Scheduled maintenance to prevent breakdowns
- **Corrective Maintenance**: Repair breakdowns and failures
- **Predictive Maintenance**: Monitor equipment condition
- **Spare Parts Management**: Maintain spare parts inventory
- **Maintenance Records**: Document all maintenance activities

### 2. Utility Management
- **Water System**: Purified water for production
- **Electricity**: Power supply and backup (genset)
- **HVAC**: Temperature and humidity control
- **Compressed Air**: Clean compressed air for production
- **Waste Treatment**: Wastewater treatment

### 3. Calibration
- **Equipment Calibration**: pH meters, viscometers, scales, thermometers
- **Calibration Schedule**: Annual or as per requirement
- **Calibration Records**: Maintain calibration certificates
- **Out-of-Calibration**: Handle equipment out of calibration

### 4. Engineering Support
- **Equipment Installation**: Install new equipment
- **Process Improvement**: Improve production processes
- **Troubleshooting**: Solve technical problems
- **Training**: Train operators on equipment use

## Maintenance Types

### Preventive Maintenance (PM)
- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, annual
- **Activities**: Cleaning, lubrication, inspection, adjustment
- **Goal**: Prevent breakdowns and extend equipment life

### Corrective Maintenance (CM)
- **Trigger**: Equipment breakdown or failure
- **Response Time**: <2 hours for critical equipment
- **Activities**: Diagnosis, repair, testing, documentation

### Predictive Maintenance (PdM)
- **Methods**: Vibration analysis, thermal imaging, oil analysis
- **Goal**: Predict failures before they occur
- **Benefit**: Reduce unplanned downtime
