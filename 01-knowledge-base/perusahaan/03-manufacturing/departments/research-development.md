- Support all 14 brands with R&D

### Core Functions

2. **Product Innovation** - Trend-based innovation
3. **Stability Testing** - Product stability evaluation
4. **Efficacy Testing** - Product performance testing

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Formula Development
- **New Product Development**: Create new formulations
- **Formula Optimization**: Improve existing formulas
- **Ingredient Research**: Research new ingredients
- **Cost Optimization**: Reduce formula costs without compromising quality
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensure formulas comply with regulations

### 2. Product Testing
- **Stability Testing**: Accelerated and long-term stability
- **Compatibility Testing**: Packaging compatibility
- **Safety Testing**: Skin irritation, sensitization
- **Efficacy Testing**: Product performance claims
- **Sensory Evaluation**: Texture, odor, appearance

### 3. Technical Documentation
- **Formula Specifications**: Detailed formula specs
- **Manufacturing Procedures**: Production instructions
- **Test Methods**: Analytical methods
- **Technical Reports**: R&D reports and studies
- **BPOM Documentation**: Registration documents

### 4. Innovation
- **Trend Analysis**: Monitor cosmetic trends
- **Competitive Analysis**: Analyze competitor products
- **New Technologies**: Explore new technologies
- **Ingredient Innovation**: Test new ingredients
- **Concept Development**: Develop product concepts

## Product Development Process

### Stage 1: Concept (1-2 weeks)
- Market research
- Concept development
- Feasibility study
- Budget estimation

### Stage 2: Formulation (4-8 weeks)
- Formula development
- Lab-scale testing
- Formula optimization
- Sample preparation

### Stage 3: Testing (8-16 weeks)
- Stability testing (accelerated)
- Safety testing
- Efficacy testing
- Sensory evaluation

### Stage 4: Scale-Up (2-4 weeks)
- Pilot batch production
- Process optimization
- Manufacturing procedure
- Training for production

### Stage 5: Registration (12-24 weeks)
- BPOM registration
- Halal certification
- Documentation preparation
- Regulatory submission

**Total Timeline**: 6-12 months from concept to market
