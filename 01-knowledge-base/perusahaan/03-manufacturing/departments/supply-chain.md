# Supply Chain

## Quick Facts
- **Type**: Manufacturing Department
- **Parent**: Lunaray Beauty Factory
- **Head**: Head of Supply Chain
- **Team Size**: 10-12 people

## Department Overview

### Vision
Memastikan supply chain yang efisien, reliable, dan cost-effective untuk mendukung produksi berkelanjutan.

### Mission
- Ensure timely procurement of raw materials
- Optimize inventory levels
- Manage warehouse operations efficiently
- Coordinate logistics and delivery
- Minimize supply chain costs

### Core Functions
1. **Procurement** - Raw material and packaging purchasing
2. **Warehouse Management** - Storage and inventory control
3. **Logistics** - Inbound and outbound logistics
4. **Vendor Management** - Supplier relationships
5. **Inventory Control** - Stock level optimization

## Team Structure

### Procurement Officer / Buyer (2 people)
### Inventory & Warehouse Staff (3-4 people)
### Warehouse Picker / Loader (2-3 people)
### Stock Opname Officer (1 person)
### Logistic & Delivery Staff (2-3 people)
### Forklift Operator (1 person)

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Procurement
- **Raw Materials**: Active ingredients, excipients
- **Packaging Materials**: Bottles, tubes, jars, boxes, labels
- **Consumables**: Cleaning supplies, PPE, office supplies
- **Vendor Sourcing**: Find and qualify suppliers
- **Price Negotiation**: Get best prices and terms
- **Purchase Orders**: Issue and track POs

### 2. Warehouse Management
- **Receiving**: Incoming material inspection and storage
- **Storage**: Proper storage conditions (temperature, humidity)
- **Inventory Control**: Stock tracking and management
- **FIFO/FEFO**: First-in-first-out / First-expired-first-out
- **Stock Opname**: Regular inventory counts

### 3. Logistics
- **Inbound Logistics**: Raw material delivery coordination
- **Outbound Logistics**: Finished goods delivery
- **Transportation**: Arrange transportation
- **Delivery Tracking**: Monitor deliveries
- **Returns Management**: Handle returns and exchanges

### 4. Vendor Management
- **Supplier Qualification**: Evaluate and approve suppliers
- **Performance Monitoring**: Track supplier performance
- **Relationship Management**: Maintain good relationships
- **Contract Negotiation**: Negotiate terms and contracts
- **Supplier Audits**: Conduct supplier audits

## Warehouse Operations

### Raw Material Warehouse
- **Capacity**: 500+ SKUs
- **Storage**: Temperature-controlled areas
- **Organization**: By category and FIFO
- **Security**: Access control, CCTV

### Packaging Material Warehouse
- **Capacity**: 300+ SKUs
- **Storage**: Dry, clean environment
- **Organization**: By product and size
- **Quality**: Protect from damage

### Finished Goods Warehouse
- **Capacity**: 50,000+ units
- **Storage**: Temperature-controlled
- **Organization**: By brand and product
- **Ready to Ship**: Packed and labeled

## Inventory Management

### Stock Levels
- **Safety Stock**: 1-2 months supply
- **Reorder Point**: Trigger for new orders
- **Maximum Stock**: Avoid overstocking
- **Minimum Stock**: Prevent stockouts

### Inventory Turnover
- **Raw Materials**: 6-8 turns/year
- **Packaging**: 8-10 turns/year
- **Finished Goods**: 10-12 turns/year

## KPIs & Metrics
- **On-Time Delivery (Suppliers)**: 95%+
- **Stockout Rate**: <2%
- **Inventory Accuracy**: 98%+
- **Warehouse Utilization**: 75-85%
- **Procurement Lead Time**: <14 days average
- **Cost Savings**: 5-10% YoY
