## Production Process

### 1. Pre-Production
- Work order review
- Raw material preparation
- Equipment setup and cleaning
- Line clearance check

### 2. Mixing/Compounding
- Formula preparation
- Ingredient weighing
- Mixing process
- Quality check (pH, viscosity, color)

### 3. Filling
- Machine setup
- Filling process
- Weight check
- In-process quality control

### 4. Packaging
- Primary packaging (bottle, tube, jar)
- Secondary packaging (box, carton)
- Labeling
- Final inspection

### 5. Post-Production
- Batch documentation
- Equipment cleaning
- Waste disposal
- Production reporting
