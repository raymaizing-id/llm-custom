## Department Overview

### Vision
Menciptakan lingkungan kerja yang aman, sehat, dan compliant dengan semua regulasi.

### Mission
- Ensure workplace safety and health
- Prevent accidents and incidents
- Ensure environmental compliance
- Maintain GMP/CPKB/CPOTB compliance
- Build safety culture

### Core Functions
1. **Health & Safety (K3)** - Workplace safety management
2. **Environmental Management** - Environmental compliance
3. **GMP Compliance** - Good Manufacturing Practice
4. **Training & Awareness** - Safety training programs
5. **Incident Management** - Accident investigation and prevention

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Health & Safety (K3)
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify and assess workplace hazards
- **Safety Procedures**: Develop and implement safety procedures
- **PPE Management**: Ensure proper use of personal protective equipment
- **Safety Inspections**: Regular workplace safety inspections
- **Incident Investigation**: Investigate accidents and near-misses

### 2. Environmental Management
- **Waste Management**: Proper disposal of waste
- **Wastewater Treatment**: Ensure wastewater compliance
- **Air Quality**: Monitor air emissions
- **Environmental Permits**: Maintain environmental permits
- **Environmental Audits**: Conduct environmental audits

### 3. GMP Compliance
- **CPKB Compliance**: Cara Pembuatan Kosmetik yang Baik
- **CPOTB Compliance**: Cara Pembuatan Obat Tradisional yang Baik
- **Documentation**: Maintain GMP documentation
- **Internal Audits**: Conduct GMP audits
- **Regulatory Inspections**: Support BPOM inspections

### 4. Training & Awareness
- **Safety Training**: Regular safety training for all employees
- **Emergency Response**: Fire drill, evacuation drill
- **First Aid Training**: First aid and CPR training
- **Safety Awareness**: Safety campaigns and communications
- **Contractor Safety**: Safety orientation for contractors

### 5. Emergency Preparedness
- **Emergency Response Plan**: Develop and maintain ERP
- **Fire Safety**: Fire prevention and firefighting
- **Evacuation Plan**: Emergency evacuation procedures
- **Emergency Equipment**: Fire extinguishers, first aid kits, eyewash stations
- **Emergency Drills**: Regular emergency drills

## Safety Programs

### Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- **Mandatory PPE**: Hairnet, mask, gloves, safety shoes, lab coat
- **PPE Training**: Proper use and maintenance
- **PPE Inspection**: Regular PPE condition check

### Chemical Safety
- **SDS Management**: Safety Data Sheets for all chemicals
- **Chemical Storage**: Proper chemical storage
- **Spill Response**: Chemical spill procedures
- **Chemical Handling**: Safe handling procedures

### Machine Safety
- **Machine Guarding**: All machines properly guarded
- **Lockout/Tagout**: LOTO procedures for maintenance
- **Machine Training**: Operator training on safe machine use

### Fire Safety
- **Fire Prevention**: Eliminate fire hazards
- **Fire Detection**: Smoke detectors, fire alarms
- **Fire Suppression**: Fire extinguishers, sprinklers
- **Fire Drills**: Quarterly fire drills

## Compliance Standards

### Regulatory Compliance
- **BPOM**: Badan Pengawas Obat dan Makanan
- **CPKB**: Cara Pembuatan Kosmetik yang Baik (GMP for Cosmetics)
- **CPOTB**: Cara Pembuatan Obat Tradisional yang Baik (GMP for Traditional Medicine)
- **K3**: Keselamatan dan Kesehatan Kerja (Occupational Health & Safety)
- **Environmental**: Ministry of Environment regulations

### Certifications
- **CPKB Certificate**: Valid and up-to-date
- **CPOTB Certificate**: Valid and up-to-date
- **Halal Certificate**: MUI Halal certification
- **ISO 9001**: Quality Management System (optional)

## KPIs & Metrics
- **Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate (LTIFR)**: 0 (zero target)
- **Near Miss Reports**: 10+ per month (encourage reporting)
- **Safety Training Hours**: 20+ hours per employee/year
- **Safety Audit Score**: 90%+
- **GMP Audit Score**: 95%+
- **Environmental Compliance**: 100%

## Incident Management

### Incident Types
- **Accident**: Injury or property damage
- **Near Miss**: Potential accident that didn't occur
- **Environmental Incident**: Spill, emission, etc.
- **Compliance Violation**: Non-compliance with regulations

### Incident Response
1. **Immediate Response**: Ensure safety, provide first aid
2. **Reporting**: Report incident to HSE department
3. **Investigation**: Investigate root cause
4. **Corrective Action**: Implement corrective actions
5. **Follow-up**: Verify effectiveness of actions
