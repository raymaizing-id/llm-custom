## 📦 SERVICES & TESTING

### 1. Product Safety Testing

#### A. Microbiological Testing
**Tests Performed**:
- Total Plate Count (TPC)
- Yeast & Mold Count
- Pathogen testing (E.coli, S.aureus, P.aeruginosa)
- Preservative efficacy test (Challenge test)
- Sterility testing

**Standards**: SNI, ISO, ASEAN Cosmetic Directive

**Turnaround Time**: 7-14 days

**Pricing**:
- Basic Micro Test: Rp 2.5 juta
- Complete Micro Panel: Rp 5 juta
- Challenge Test: Rp 8 juta

---

#### B. Heavy Metal Testing
**Metals Tested**:
- Lead (Pb)
- Mercury (Hg)

- Cadmium (Cd)

**Turnaround Time**: 10-14 days

**Pricing**:
