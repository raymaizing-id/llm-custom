## 10. CONTENT REPURPOSING FRAMEWORK

### n8n Workflow for Article Repurposing
Based on workflow diagram yang disebutkan, berikut adalah content knowledge untuk repurposing system:

#### Input Article Requirements
**Original Article Specifications:**
- Length: 1500-3000 words (optimal untuk repurposing)
- Structure: Clear headings, subheadings, key points
- Topics: Business, technology, marketing, beauty industry, education, entrepreneurship
- Tone: Professional, informative, engaging
- SEO: Keyword-optimized, meta description included

#### Output Content Variations (10+ Versions)

**1. Brand Voice & Tone Adaptation (3 versions):**
- **Corporate/Professional Version:** Formal language, data-focused, suitable untuk B2B audiences dan corporate communications
- **Conversational Version:** Friendly tone, relatable examples, personal pronouns, suitable untuk social media dan blog
- **Perspective Article Version:** Opinion-driven, thought leadership angle, first-person narrative, industry commentary

**2. Platform-Specific Adaptations (7+ versions):**

**A. Social Media Posts:**
- **LinkedIn Post:** Professional insights, industry relevance, 1300-2000 characters, includes key statistics, thought-provoking question, 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Instagram Caption:** Visual storytelling approach, 150-300 characters main text + extended caption option, emoji usage, strong hook, call-to-action, 15-25 hashtags
- **Twitter/X Thread:** 8-12 tweet thread, each tweet 200-280 characters, numbered for easy reading, key insights breakdown, thread conclusion with CTA
- **Facebook Post:** Conversational tone, community-focused, 200-500 words, storytelling element, engagement question, shareable format

**B. Blog/Article Formats:**
- **Short-Form Blog (800-1000 words):** Key highlights dari original, faster read, single focus point, clear takeaway
- **List Article:** "10 Ways to...", "5 Strategies for...", scannable format, actionable tips, brief explanations
- **How-To Guide:** Step-by-step instructions, practical application, examples included, clear process flow
- **Case Study Format:** Problem-solution structure, real-world example, results-focused, data points included

**C. Email Marketing:**
- **Newsletter Version:** 400-600 words, conversational opener, 2-3 key points, links to full article, clear CTA, PS with additional value
- **Email Sequence (3 emails):** 
  - Email 1: Introduction & problem statement
  - Email 2: Solution & insights
  - Email 3: Call-to-action & next steps
