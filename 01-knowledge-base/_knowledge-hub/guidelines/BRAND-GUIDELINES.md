# 🎨 Brand Guidelines
## Comprehensive Brand Standards for RayCorp

> **Purpose**: Brand guidelines untuk semua entities di RayCorp  
> **Status**: Active  
> **Last Updated**: 2026-03-12

---

## 🎯 BRAND IDENTITY

### Brand Values
- **Innovation**: Continuous improvement
- **Quality**: Excellence in everything
- **Integrity**: Honest and transparent
- **Customer Focus**: Customer success first
- **Sustainability**: Responsible business

### Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Professional yet approachable
- **Personality**: Innovative, trustworthy, helpful
- **Language**: Clear, concise, jargon-free
- **Style**: Consistent across all channels

### Visual Identity
- **Logo**: Use approved logo only
- **Colors**: Primary: Blue, Secondary: Green
- **Typography**: Sans-serif for headers, serif for body
- **Imagery**: Professional, diverse, authentic
- **Spacing**: Consistent margins and padding

---

## 📝 COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

### Written Communication
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short
- Use simple language
- Avoid jargon
- Proofread carefully

### Visual Communication
- Use high-quality images
- Maintain consistent style
- Include diverse representation
- Use brand colors
- Follow layout guidelines

### Social Media
- Be authentic and genuine
- Respond to comments
- Share valuable content
- Engage with community
- Stay on brand

---

## 🚫 BRAND MISUSE

### Don't
- Alter logo or colors
- Use outdated branding
- Mix brand voices
- Use low-quality images
- Ignore guidelines

### Do
- Use approved assets
- Follow guidelines
- Maintain consistency
- Use high-quality content
- Ask if unsure
