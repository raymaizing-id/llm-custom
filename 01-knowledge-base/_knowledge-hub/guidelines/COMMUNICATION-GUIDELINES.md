## 📧 EMAIL COMMUNICATION

### Email Best Practices
- **Subject line**: Clear and specific
- **Greeting**: Professional and warm
- **Body**: Concise and organized
- **CTA**: Clear call to action
- **Signature**: Professional signature
- **Response time**: Reply within 24 hours

### Email Structure
1. Greeting
2. Purpose statement
3. Key information
4. Call to action
5. Closing
6. Signature

---

## 📞 PHONE COMMUNICATION

### Phone Etiquette
- Answer within 3 rings
- Identify yourself
- Listen actively
- Take notes
- Follow up in writing
- Professional tone

---

## 💻 DIGITAL COMMUNICATION

### Slack/Teams
- Professional tone
- Timely responses
- Clear messages
- Use threads
- Respect working hours

### Video Calls
- Professional background
- Good lighting
- Mute when not speaking
- Make eye contact
- Dress appropriately

---

## 🎤 PRESENTATIONS

### Presentation Guidelines
- Clear structure
- Visual aids
- Engaging delivery
- Time management
- Q&A preparation
