## 📞 COMMUNICATION BEST PRACTICES

### Response Time
- Email: Within 24 hours
- Chat: Within 5 minutes
- Phone: Answer within 3 rings

### Communication Style
- Professional yet friendly
- Clear and concise
- Empathetic tone
- Solution-focused
- Positive language

### Escalation
- Know when to escalate
- Provide context
- Follow procedures
- Keep customer informed
- Ensure resolution
