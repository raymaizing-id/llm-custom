# 💼 Sales Process

> **Status**: Active

## 🎯 SALES STAGES

### Stage 1: Prospecting
**Objective**: Identify potential customers
- Research target companies
- Build prospect list
- Prepare outreach
- Initial contact
- **Duration**: 1-2 weeks

### Stage 2: Qualification
**Objective**: Determine if prospect is qualified
- Assess fit
- Identify needs
- Understand budget
- Confirm timeline
- **Duration**: 1-2 weeks

### Stage 3: Discovery
**Objective**: Understand customer needs deeply
- Schedule discovery call
- Ask discovery questions
- Identify pain points
- Understand goals
- **Duration**: 1-2 weeks

### Stage 4: Proposal
**Objective**: Present solution
- Prepare proposal
- Present solution
- Address questions
- Negotiate terms
- **Duration**: 1-2 weeks

### Stage 5: Negotiation
**Objective**: Finalize terms
- Discuss pricing
- Negotiate terms
- Address concerns
- Prepare contract
- **Duration**: 1-2 weeks

### Stage 6: Closing
**Objective**: Secure commitment
- Final review
- Get signature
- Confirm details
- Schedule kickoff
- **Duration**: 1 week

### Stage 7: Onboarding
**Objective**: Successful implementation
- Introduce support team
- Set expectations
- Begin implementation
- Schedule check-ins
- **Duration**: 2-4 weeks

---

## 📊 SALES METRICS

### Key Metrics
- Win rate: % of deals closed
- Average deal size: Revenue per deal
- Sales cycle: Days from prospect to close
- CAC: Cost to acquire customer
- CLV: Customer lifetime value
