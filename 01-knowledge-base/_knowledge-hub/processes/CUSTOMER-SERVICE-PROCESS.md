# 🎯 Customer Service Process
## Standard Customer Service Workflow

> **Purpose**: Standardized customer service process  
> **Status**: Active  
> **Last Updated**: 2026-03-12

---

## 🔄 SERVICE WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Receive Request
- Customer contacts support
- Log request in system
- Assign ticket number
- Acknowledge receipt

### Step 2: Triage
- Assess urgency
- Categorize issue
- Assign to specialist
- Set priority

### Step 3: Investigation
- Gather information
- Understand problem
- Research solution
- Consult resources

### Step 4: Resolution
- Implement solution
- Test fix
- Verify resolution
- Document solution

### Step 5: Follow-up
- Confirm satisfaction
- Gather feedback
- Close ticket
- Schedule follow-up

---

## 📊 SERVICE METRICS

### Track
- Response time
- Resolution time
- Customer satisfaction
- First contact resolution
- Repeat contact rate
