## 👔 WORKPLACE CONDUCT

### Professional Behavior
- Treat everyone with respect
- Maintain professional environment
- Follow company values
- Report violations
- Zero tolerance for harassment

### Attendance & Punctuality
- Arrive on time
- Notify manager if late
- Maintain attendance record
- Request time off in advance
- Follow company schedule

### Dress Code
- Professional attire
- Clean and neat
- Appropriate for role
- Client-facing: Business formal
- Office: Business casual

---

## 🔐 CONFIDENTIALITY

### Confidential Information
- Don't share company secrets
- Protect customer data
- Secure documents
- Use secure passwords
- Report breaches immediately

### Data Protection
- Follow GDPR/local regulations
- Encrypt sensitive data
- Limit access
- Regular backups
- Incident response plan

---

## 💼 CONFLICT OF INTEREST

### Disclosure
- Disclose conflicts
- Avoid self-dealing
- Recuse yourself if needed
- Document decisions
- Seek approval

### Prohibited Activities
- Competing with company
- Accepting bribes
- Insider trading
- Nepotism
- Favoritism

## 🚫 PROHIBITED CONDUCT

### Zero Tolerance
- Harassment
- Discrimination
- Violence
- Theft
- Fraud
