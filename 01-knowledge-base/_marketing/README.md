# Marketing Resources

This folder contains marketing resources, campaigns, content, and strategies across the RayCorp ecosystem.

## Structure

```
_marketing/
├── campaigns/      # Marketing campaigns (past and current)
├── content/        # Marketing content and copy
├── strategies/     # Marketing strategies and plans
└── assets/         # Marketing assets (links to media files)
```

## Contents

### Campaigns
- Campaign briefs and objectives
- Campaign timelines and milestones
- Performance metrics and results
- Multi-channel campaign coordination

### Content
- Marketing copy and messaging
- Social media content
- Email marketing templates
- Blog posts and articles
- Video scripts

### Strategies
- Brand positioning strategies
- Go-to-market plans
- Channel strategies
- Customer acquisition strategies
- Content marketing strategies

### Assets
- References to visual assets (stored in `99-area/media/`)
- Brand guidelines
- Design templates
- Asset libraries

## Marketing Scope

Marketing resources cover:
- **All Business Units**: Lunaranger, Dianranger, Raycademy, Agroranger, Cosranger
- **All Brands**: B2C, B2B, B2B2C brands
- **All Products**: Across all categories
- **All Channels**: Digital, traditional, social, events

## Cross-Reference

Marketing connects:
- `_brands/` - Brands being marketed
- `_products/` - Products being promoted
- `unit-bisnis/` - Business units executing marketing
- `99-area/media/` - Media assets used in marketing
