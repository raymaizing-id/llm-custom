This folder contains marketing content, copy, and messaging across all channels.

## Content Types

### Social Media
- Post copy
- Captions
- Hashtag strategies
- Content calendars

### Email Marketing
- Email templates
- Newsletter content
- Drip campaign sequences
- Promotional emails

### Website Content
- Landing page copy
- Product descriptions
- About us content
- Blog posts

### Video Content
- Video scripts
- Storyboards
- Video descriptions
- YouTube content

### Advertising
- Ad copy
- Ad variations
- A/B test content
- Campaign messaging

## Content Organization

Organize by:
- Channel (social, email, web, video, ads)
- Brand or business unit
- Campaign association
