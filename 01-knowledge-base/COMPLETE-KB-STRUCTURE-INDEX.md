2. **EXTERNAL-KB**: Untuk customers, prospects, public (customer-facing)

## 📁 FILE STRUCTURE & NAMING CONVENTION

### Naming Convention Rules:
1. **Internal Files**: `INTERNAL-KB-[ENTITY-NAME].md`
2. **External Files**: `EXTERNAL-KB-[ENTITY-NAME].md`
3. **Division AI Files**: `AI-KB-[DIVISION-NAME].md`
4. **Entity name**: UPPERCASE, no spaces (use dash)

### Why This Convention?
- ✅ Clear distinction between internal/external
- ✅ Easy to identify in n8n workflows
- ✅ No conflicts in custom AI systems
- ✅ Searchable and organized
- ✅ Scalable for future additions
