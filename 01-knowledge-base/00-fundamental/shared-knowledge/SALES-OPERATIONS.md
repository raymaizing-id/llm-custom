## 📦 INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

### 1. Stock Level Optimization

**Stock Level Calculation**:
- Minimum stock: 2 weeks sales
- Maximum stock: 8 weeks sales
- Reorder point: 3 weeks sales
- Safety stock: 1 week sales

**Inventory Monitoring**:
- Daily: Fast-moving items (top 20%)
- Weekly: Regular items
- Monthly: Slow-moving items
- Quarterly: Dead stock review

---

### 2. Reorder Point Calculation

**Formula**:
```
Reorder Point = (Average Daily Sales × Lead Time) + Safety Stock
```

**Example**:
- Average daily sales: 50 units
- Lead time: 7 days
- Safety stock: 100 units
- Reorder point: (50 × 7) + 100 = 450 units

---

### 3. Safety Stock Determination

**Safety Stock Factors**:
- Demand variability
- Supply variability
- Service level target
- Holding cost
- Stockout cost

**Service Level**:
- 90% service level: Lower safety stock
- 95% service level: Medium safety stock
- 99% service level: Higher safety stock

---

## 💵 PRICING STRATEGY

### 1. Cost Analysis

**Cost Components**:
- Product cost (COGS)
- Packaging cost
- Shipping cost
- Marketing cost
- Overhead cost
- Profit margin

**Cost Calculation**:
```
Total Cost = COGS + Packaging + Shipping + Marketing + Overhead
Profit = Revenue - Total Cost
Margin = (Profit / Revenue) × 100%
```

---

### 2. Competitive Pricing

**Competitive Analysis**:
- Identify competitors
- Analyze their pricing
- Understand their positioning
- Identify pricing gaps
- Benchmark performance

**Pricing Positioning**:
- Premium pricing (higher than competitors)
- Competitive pricing (similar to competitors)
- Value pricing (lower than competitors)

---

### 3. Value-Based Pricing

**Value Assessment**:

## 📊 CUSTOMIZATION GUIDELINES

### How to Adapt for B2C Brands

**Customization Points**:
- **Channels**: E-commerce focus (70%+)
- **Pricing**: Consumer-friendly pricing
- **Promotions**: Frequent, attractive offers
- **Inventory**: Higher turnover
- **Customer**: Individual consumers

**Example - Adhwa (B2C)**:
- Channels: 60% online, 40% offline
- Price range: Rp 75K - 175K
- Promotion: Ramadan, Eid, seasonal
- Inventory: 2-8 weeks supply
- Target: Rp 4.2B/month

---

### How to Adapt for B2B Brands

**Customization Points**:
- **Channels**: Direct sales, partnerships
- **Pricing**: Volume-based, contract pricing
- **Promotions**: Seasonal, volume-based
- **Inventory**: Larger quantities
- **Customer**: Business buyers

---

### How to Adapt for B2B2C Brands

**Customization Points**:
- **Channels**: Dual channel (B2B + B2C)
- **Pricing**: Different for each channel
- **Promotions**: Tailored per channel
- **Inventory**: Balanced allocation
- **Customer**: Both business & consumer
