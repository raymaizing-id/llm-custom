## 🎯 PLATFORM STRATEGY

### 1. Platform Selection Criteria

**Evaluation Criteria**:
- **Audience presence**: Where is target audience?
- **Content fit**: What content works on platform?
- **Engagement potential**: How engaged is audience?
- **Resource requirement**: How much effort needed?
- **ROI potential**: What's the return?
- **Competitive presence**: Are competitors there?

**Platform Characteristics**:

| Platform | Audience | Content | Engagement | Best For |
|----------|----------|---------|------------|----------|
| Instagram | 18-45, visual | Photos, reels, stories | High | Visual brands, lifestyle |
| TikTok | 13-35, trends | Short videos, trends | Very high | Young audience, viral |
| Facebook | 25-65, broad | Posts, videos, groups | Medium | Community, events, ads |
| YouTube | All ages | Long-form video | High | Tutorials, vlogs, education |
| LinkedIn | 25-65, B2B | Articles, insights | Medium | B2B, thought leadership |
| WhatsApp | All ages, messaging | Messages, groups | High | Community, customer service |
| Twitter/X | 18-45, news | Tweets, threads | High | News, engagement, trends |

---

### 2. Platform-Specific Best Practices

**Instagram**:
- **Feed**: High-quality images, consistent aesthetic
- **Stories**: Daily updates, behind-the-scenes, polls
- **Reels**: Trending audio, entertaining, 15-60 seconds
- **DM**: Quick responses, personal touch
- **Hashtags**: 20-30 relevant hashtags
- **Posting time**: 11 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM (test for audience)
- **Frequency**: 1-2 posts/day + 5-10 stories

**TikTok**:
- **Trends**: Use trending sounds, challenges
- **Format**: Vertical, fast-paced, entertaining
- **Length**: 15-60 seconds optimal
- **Authenticity**: Raw, unpolished content performs well
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Posting time**: 6-10 AM, 7-11 PM
- **Frequency**: 1-3 videos/day

**Facebook**:
- **Posts**: Mix of content types
- **Videos**: Auto-play, captions
- **Groups**: Community building, engagement
- **Events**: Event promotion, RSVP
- **Ads**: Detailed targeting, retargeting
- **Posting time**: 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Frequency**: 1-2 posts/day

**YouTube**:
- **Channel**: Professional branding, clear description
- **Videos**: High production quality, 10+ minutes
- **Thumbnails**: Eye-catching, consistent style
- **Titles**: SEO-optimized, compelling
- **Descriptions**: Links, timestamps, keywords
- **Upload schedule**: Consistent (weekly or bi-weekly)
- **Frequency**: 1-2 videos/week

**LinkedIn**:
- **Posts**: Professional, insightful, industry-relevant
- **Articles**: Long-form thought leadership
- **Videos**: Professional, educational
- **Engagement**: Comment on others' posts
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 industry hashtags
- **Posting time**: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM (weekdays)
- **Frequency**: 3-5 posts/week

**WhatsApp**:
- **Groups**: Community building, customer service
- **Broadcasts**: One-way messaging
- **Status**: Daily updates, stories
- **Response time**: <5 minutes
- **Tone**: Personal, friendly, helpful
- **Frequency**: Daily engagement

## 📅 CONTENT CALENDAR MANAGEMENT

### 1. Content Calendar Creation

**Calendar Structure**:
- **Monthly view**: Themes, campaigns, events
- **Weekly view**: Daily topics, content types
- **Daily view**: Specific posts, timing, platforms

**Planning Timeline**:
- Month -1: Plan monthly themes
- Week -2: Plan weekly topics
- Week -1: Create content
- Week 0: Schedule posts
- Week +1: Monitor & adjust

**Content Planning Process**:
1. Define monthly theme
2. Plan weekly topics
3. Create content (2-4 weeks ahead)
4. Schedule posts (1-2 weeks ahead)
5. Monitor performance (real-time)

## 🤝 INFLUENCER PARTNERSHIPS

### 1. Influencer Identification

**Influencer Tiers**:
- **Macro** (100K-1M followers): Broad reach, lower engagement
- **Micro** (10K-100K followers): Good reach, high engagement
- **Nano** (1K-10K followers): Small reach, very high engagement
- **Celebrity**: High reach, high cost

**Selection Criteria**:
- Audience alignment
- Engagement rate (not just followers)
- Authenticity
- Brand fit
- Previous work quality
- Audience demographics
- Engagement quality

**Influencer Research**:
- Analyze audience demographics
- Check engagement rate
- Review previous partnerships
- Check audience authenticity
- Verify brand alignment

**B2B/Services**:

- Platforms: LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter
- Content: Insights, case studies, thought leadership
- Influencers: Industry experts

**Food/Beverage**:
- Focus: Visual, appetizing content
- Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- Content: Food photos, recipes, behind-the-scenes
- Influencers: Food bloggers, chefs

**Technology**:
- Focus: Educational, innovative
- Platforms: YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter
- Content: Tutorials, product demos, industry news
- Influencers: Tech reviewers, thought leaders

- Frequency: High

**Adult Audience (25-45)**:
- Platforms: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn

- Frequency: Medium

**Mature Audience (45+)**:
- Platforms: Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn
- Content: Educational, informative, practical
- Tone: Professional, respectful, clear
- Frequency: Lower
