## 🎯 CONTENT STRATEGY

### 1. Content Objectives

**Content Goals**:
- **Awareness**: Reach new audience
- **Education**: Teach audience
- **Engagement**: Build community
- **Conversion**: Drive sales
- **Retention**: Keep customers
- **Advocacy**: Generate referrals

**Alignment with Business**:
- Support marketing objectives
- Support sales objectives
- Support customer service
- Support brand positioning

---

### 2. Content Types

**Written Content**:
- Blog posts (1,000-3,000 words)
- Articles (2,000-5,000 words)
- Case studies (2,000-4,000 words)
- Whitepapers (5,000-10,000 words)
- Email newsletters
- Social media captions
- Product descriptions

**Visual Content**:
- Images (product, lifestyle, behind-the-scenes)
- Infographics (data visualization)
- Carousels (multi-image posts)
- Memes (trending, relatable)
- Screenshots (tips, tutorials)
- Diagrams (process, structure)

**Video Content**:
- Short-form (15-60 seconds): TikTok, Reels, Shorts
- Medium-form (2-5 minutes): YouTube, social media
- Long-form (10+ minutes): YouTube, webinars
- Live video: Instagram Live, Facebook Live
- Testimonials: Customer stories
- Tutorials: How-to, product demos

**Interactive Content**:
- Quizzes (engagement, lead generation)
- Polls (engagement, feedback)
- Contests (engagement, reach)
- Surveys (feedback, insights)
- Calculators (utility, lead generation)

---

### 3. Content Themes & Pillars

**Content Pillars** (3-5 main themes):
1. **Pillar 1**: Core value/mission
2. **Pillar 2**: Product/service benefits
3. **Pillar 3**: Industry insights
4. **Pillar 4**: Customer stories
5. **Pillar 5**: Brand personality

**Content Themes**:
- Educational: Teach audience something valuable
- Inspirational: Motivate, empower audience
- Entertaining: Make audience laugh, enjoy
- Promotional: Sell products/services
- Community: Build relationships, engagement

---

### 4. Content Calendar

**Calendar Structure**:
- **Monthly view**: Themes, campaigns, events
- **Weekly view**: Daily topics, content types
- **Daily view**: Specific posts, timing, platforms

**Planning Timeline**:
- Month -1: Plan monthly themes
- Week -2: Plan weekly topics
- Week -1: Create content
- Week 0: Schedule posts
- Week +1: Monitor & adjust

---

### 5. Audience Insights

**Audience Analysis**:
- Demographics (age, gender, location)
- Psychographics (values, interests, lifestyle)
- Behavior (online habits, preferences)
- Pain points (problems, challenges)
- Aspirations (goals, desires)

**Content Preferences**:

### 6. Messaging Framework

**Core Message**:

**Supporting Messages** (3-5):

---

### 5. Content Brief Creation

**Content Brief Elements**:
- Topic/title
- Target audience
- Key messages
- Content type
- Format/length
- Keywords
- Call to action
- Deadline
- Approval process

**Content Brief Template**:
```
Title: [Topic]
Audience: [Target audience]
Objective: [What should this achieve?]
Key Messages: [3-5 main points]
Format: [Blog, video, infographic, etc.]
Length: [Word count or duration]
Keywords: [3-5 target keywords]
CTA: [Call to action]
Deadline: [Due date]
```

---

### 6. Timeline Planning

**Content Timeline**:
- Ideation: 1-2 days
- Research: 2-3 days
- Brief creation: 1 day
- Content creation: 3-5 days
- Review: 1-2 days
- Revisions: 1-2 days
- Scheduling: 1 day
- Publishing: 1 day

**Total**: 10-16 days per piece

## ✍️ CONTENT CREATION

### 1. Writing Guidelines

**Writing Best Practices**:
- Clear, concise language
- Active voice
- Short sentences
- Short paragraphs
- Scannable format
- Compelling headlines
- Strong opening
- Clear CTA

**SEO Writing**:
- Include target keyword in title
- Include keyword in first 100 words
- Use keyword naturally (2-3 times)
- Use related keywords
- Include internal links
- Optimize meta description

---

### 2. Visual Design Guidelines

**Design Principles**:
- Consistent branding
- Clear hierarchy
- Readable fonts
- Appropriate colors
- High-quality images
- Proper spacing
- Mobile-friendly

**Image Guidelines**:
- High resolution (1200x630px minimum)
- Consistent style
- Relevant to content
- Optimized for web
- Alt text for accessibility

---

### 3. Video Production Guidelines

**Video Best Practices**:
- Clear audio
- Good lighting
- Stable camera
- Engaging opening (first 3 seconds)
- Clear messaging
- Captions/subtitles
- Call to action
- Proper length

**Video Optimization**:
- Vertical format (for mobile)
- Thumbnail optimization
- Title optimization
- Description optimization
- Tags optimization

---

### 4. Photography Guidelines

**Photography Best Practices**:
- Professional quality
- Consistent style
- Relevant to content
- Authentic (not overly staged)
- Diverse representation
- Proper lighting
- Clear subject

**Photo Editing**:
- Consistent filters/style
- Proper color correction
- Appropriate cropping
- Watermark (if needed)
- Optimized for web

---

### 5. Audio/Music Guidelines

**Audio Best Practices**:
- Clear, professional audio
- Appropriate background music
- Proper volume levels
- Royalty-free music
- Consistent style
- Captions for accessibility

**Music Selection**:
- Royalty-free sources (Epidemic Sound, Artlist)
- Appropriate for brand
- Appropriate for content
- Proper licensing

---

### 6. Accessibility Guidelines

**Accessibility Best Practices**:
- Alt text for images
- Captions for videos
- Transcripts for audio
- Readable fonts
- Sufficient color contrast
- Keyboard navigation
- Screen reader friendly

## ✅ CONTENT REVIEW & APPROVAL

### 1. Review Process

**Review Steps**:
1. Content creator: Create content
2. Reviewer: Check quality, brand fit
3. Approver: Final approval
4. Scheduler: Schedule post
5. Monitor: Track performance

**Review Timeline**:
- Reviewer: 1-2 days
- Approver: 1 day
- Scheduler: 1 day

---

### 2. Approval Workflow

**Approval Checklist**:
- ✅ Brand guidelines followed
- ✅ Messaging correct
- ✅ No typos/grammar errors
- ✅ Images/videos high quality
- ✅ Links working
- ✅ Hashtags relevant
- ✅ CTA clear
- ✅ Timing optimal
- ✅ Accessibility compliant
- ✅ Legal/compliance reviewed

---

### 3. Feedback Incorporation

**Feedback Process**:
1. Reviewer provides feedback
2. Creator incorporates feedback
3. Reviewer approves changes
4. Move to next stage

**Feedback Types**:
- Content feedback (accuracy, clarity)
- Brand feedback (tone, messaging)
- Technical feedback (formatting, links)
- Legal feedback (compliance, accuracy)

---

### 4. Quality Checks

**Quality Checklist**:
- ✅ Accurate information
- ✅ Proper grammar/spelling
- ✅ Consistent formatting
- ✅ Proper citations
- ✅ Working links
- ✅ Proper image attribution
- ✅ Accessibility compliant

---

### 5. Compliance Checks

**Compliance Review**:
- ✅ Legal compliance
- ✅ Brand compliance
- ✅ Regulatory compliance
- ✅ Privacy compliance
- ✅ Advertising compliance

---

### 6. Final Approval

**Final Approval**:
- Approver reviews all feedback
- Approver checks quality
- Approver approves for publishing
- Content moves to scheduling

## 🎨 CUSTOMIZATION GUIDELINES

### How to Adapt for Different Content Types

**Blog Content**:
- Focus: SEO, depth, authority
- Length: 1,500-3,000 words
- Format: Structured, scannable
- Timeline: 2-3 weeks

**Video Content**:
- Focus: Engagement, entertainment
- Length: 2-5 minutes (short-form)
- Format: Visual, dynamic
- Timeline: 1-2 weeks

**Social Media Content**:
- Focus: Engagement, reach
- Length: Short, punchy
- Format: Visual, interactive
- Timeline: 1-3 days
