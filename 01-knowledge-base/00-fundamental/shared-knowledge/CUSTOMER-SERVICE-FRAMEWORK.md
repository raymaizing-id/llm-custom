## 📋 SERVICE STANDARDS

### 1. Response Time Standards

**Response Time SLAs**:
- **Chat** (WhatsApp, Messenger, Shopee, Tokopedia): <5 minutes
- **Email**: <2 hours
- **Phone**: <3 rings
- **Social media DM**: <15 minutes
- **In-store**: Immediate

**24/7 Coverage**:
- Assign team members by timezone
- Escalation procedures
- Emergency protocols
- After-hours support

---

### 2. Resolution Time Standards

**Resolution Time Targets**:
- **Simple inquiries**: <1 hour
- **Standard issues**: <24 hours
- **Complex issues**: <48 hours
- **Complaints**: <24 hours

**First Contact Resolution**:
- Resolve 90%+ of issues on first contact
- Minimize escalations
- Empower team to solve
- Provide clear authority

---

### 3. Quality Standards

**Quality Metrics**:
- **Accuracy**: Correct information provided
- **Completeness**: All questions answered
- **Professionalism**: Professional tone
- **Empathy**: Understanding customer needs
- **Efficiency**: Quick resolution

**Quality Assurance**:
- Random call/chat monitoring
- Customer satisfaction surveys
- Feedback collection
- Continuous training
- Performance reviews

---

### 4. Availability Standards

**Operating Hours**:
- Business hours: 9 AM - 6 PM (minimum)
- Extended hours: 8 AM - 9 PM (recommended)
- 24/7: For critical issues (optional)

**Channel Availability**:
- Chat: 24/7 (automated + human)
- Email: 24/7 (response within SLA)
- Phone: Business hours minimum
- Social media: Business hours minimum

---

### 5. Escalation Procedures

**Escalation Matrix**:
- **Level 1** (CS): Product info, order status, simple issues
- **Level 2** (Supervisor): Complaints, refunds, complex issues
- **Level 3** (Manager): Major complaints, brand issues
- **Level 4** (Director): Crisis situations

**Escalation Triggers**:
- Customer dissatisfaction
- Complex issue
- Complaint
- Refund request
- Legal issue
- Media attention

---

### 6. SLA Definitions

**Service Level Agreement**:
- Response time commitment
- Resolution time commitment
- Quality standards
- Availability commitment
- Escalation procedures
- Compensation for breach

## 😊 CUSTOMER SATISFACTION

### 1. Satisfaction Measurement

**Satisfaction Metrics**:
- **CSAT** (Customer Satisfaction Score): 1-5 scale
- **NPS** (Net Promoter Score): 0-10 scale
- **CES** (Customer Effort Score): 1-5 scale

**Measurement Methods**:
- Post-interaction surveys
- Email surveys
- Phone surveys
- In-app surveys
- Social media feedback

---

### 2. NPS Tracking

**NPS Calculation**:
```
NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors

Promoters (9-10): Likely to recommend
Passives (7-8): Satisfied but not enthusiastic
Detractors (0-6): Unlikely to recommend
```

**NPS Targets**:
- 0-30: Needs improvement
- 30-50: Good
- 50-70: Excellent
- 70+: World-class

---

### 3. CSAT Surveys

**CSAT Question**:
"How satisfied are you with your experience?"
- 1 = Very dissatisfied
- 5 = Very satisfied

**CSAT Target**: 4.5+/5

- Focus: Professional, detailed support
