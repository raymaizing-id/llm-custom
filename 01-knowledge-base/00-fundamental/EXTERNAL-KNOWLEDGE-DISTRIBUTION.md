## 🎯 CUSTOMER JOURNEY MAPPING (External)

### Stage 1: Discovery
**User**: Baru mengenal brand
**AI Role**: Introduce & educate
**Content**: Brand story, value prop, benefits
**CTA**: "Pelajari lebih lanjut"

### Stage 2: Research
**User**: Mencari informasi detail
**AI Role**: Provide comprehensive info
**Content**: Product details, comparisons, reviews
**CTA**: "Bandingkan produk"

### Stage 3: Consideration
**User**: Membandingkan options
**AI Role**: Help decision making
**Content**: Recommendations, testimonials, guarantees
**CTA**: "Lihat rekomendasi"

### Stage 4: Purchase
**User**: Siap membeli
**AI Role**: Facilitate transaction
**Content**: Pricing, how to order, payment
**CTA**: "Pesan sekarang"

### Stage 5: Post-Purchase
**User**: Sudah membeli
**AI Role**: Support & engage
**Content**: Usage tips, support, upsell
**CTA**: "Butuh bantuan?"

### Stage 6: Loyalty
**User**: Repeat customer
**AI Role**: Retain & reward
**Content**: Exclusive offers, community, referral
**CTA**: "Dapatkan reward"
